¿Que son?
Son represent

<lc-exp> ::= <identifier>
            var-exp(id)
         ::= "lambda" "(" <identifier>* ")" <lc-exp>
          lambda-exp(lid, exp)
         ::= "(" <lc-exp> <lc-exp> ")"
          app-exp(rator, rand)
